Why Choose OrderSune POS?

Tableside Ordering Managed at Store Level

OrderSune’s system supports traditional tableside ordering managed by the restaurant staff. This allows for a personalized touch, where servers can take orders directly at the table, enhancing customer service and interaction.

Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

Empower your customers to use their own devices to place orders. By scanning a QR code placed on the table, customers can browse the menu, customize their orders, and complete the transaction without waiting for a server. This BYOD approach enhances convenience and reduces wait times, leading to a better dining experience.

QR Code Ordering

Our QR code ordering system is simple and intuitive. Customers can scan the QR code with their smartphone, which directs them to your restaurant’s digital menu. From there, they can easily place their orders and even make payments, streamlining the entire process.

Seamless Integration with POS System

All orders placed through the tableside/BYOD/QR system are directly integrated into your POS system. This ensures accuracy and efficiency, minimizing errors and enhancing operational flow.

Real-Time Menu Updates

Our system allows for real-time updates to the menu, ensuring that any changes in availability or pricing are instantly reflected. This keeps customers informed and helps avoid confusion or dissatisfaction.

Enhanced Customer Experience

By integrating these advanced ordering methods, your restaurant can offer a modern and efficient dining experience. Customers will appreciate the convenience and speed, leading to increased satisfaction and loyalty.

Reduced Contact and Increased Safety

In a post-pandemic world, reducing contact points is crucial for safety. QR code ordering minimizes physical interactions, providing a safer dining environment for both customers and staff.
